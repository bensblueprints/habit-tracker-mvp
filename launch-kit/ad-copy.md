**Hook:** Your habit app killed a 90-day streak because you took a weekend off. 😤
**Body:** Streakly's streak engine is actually fair: weekday habits survive weekends, "3× per week" counts weekly quotas, and vacation mode freezes streaks instead of breaking them. Built for humans, not perfect robots.
**CTA:** Track habits the fair way → Streakly, $15 one-time

**Body:** Streakly keeps 365 days of habit history in a single file on YOUR machine — GitHub-style heatmaps, completion stats, CSV export for your spreadsheets. No subscription, no telemetry, MIT-licensed source.
